Instructions

How to Play ?

  • You will be given a cube which is built by multiple color blocks, and each of the sides contains 9 blocks.
  • To rotate the cube 9 buttons are availaible on the top of the cube.
  • Push random buttons to shuffle the cube in arbitary position.
  • Now bend your mind to solve this arbitary combination.
  • If you are unable to do so, you can click 'Reset' button and start the game again.

Buttons

  • "M" Moves (Left "L" or Right "R" face moves)
    • LM: M in LEFT side
    • RM: M in RIGHT side
  • "S" Moves (Front "F" or Back "B" face moves)
    • FS: S in FRONT side
    • BS: S in BACK side
  • "E" Moves (Up "U" or Down "D" face moves)
    • UE: E in UP side
    • DE: E DOWN side
