Instructions

How to Play ?

  • You will be given a cube which is built by multiple color blocks, and each of the sides contains 9 blocks.
  • To rotate the cube you can swipe on any side of the cube
  • Do random swiping to shuffle the cube in arbitary position.
  • Now bend your mind to solve this arbitary combination.
  • If you are unable to do so, you can click 'solve' button and start the game again.

Buttons

  • : Undo the steps
  • : Redo the steps
  • : Solve the cube
  • : Rotate the Cube
