About "BOOK IT!"

Welcome to "BOOK IT!"

Movie booking made so simpler now at Book It!, the most premium and leading online booking system in India! This application allows you to look up showtimes for movies running in all Cinemas near you and across India. You can get movie information and book your tickets securely just by using your mobile phone.Just imagine the convenience at your finger tips, directly from your smartphone through PayPal or with other E-Payment facilities, regardless of which telco company you subscribe to.

Features includes checking out a movie synopsis, watch high-quality movie posters, keep a track of your booking history, receiving discount offers & deals, get information on latest and upcoming releases and most importantly it's free.
